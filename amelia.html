Amelia (Alice) Wattson-Nesterov (née Amelia Wattson, 15 March 2007 - April 2049 to February 2050) was a lead character in Eternal Darkness. She was originally created by Andrew on November of 2021 and later used for the ED Roleplay Campaigns.

Amelia was a mercenary with strong tides to the Dempsey Administration of New Order.

Biography

Early Life

Her original name, parents or nationality are unknown. However, it is known that she was given to the the 'Dempsey Foundation' in 2018 when she was 11 years old and used in the [name to be decided] experiments where her caretakers (notably Dr. Isaac Marshall) reffered to her as 'Alice', a name she kept hidden most of her life. It was inside the foundation where she was experimented on in order to test the effect of the Campbell-Horn Classified Materials [discussion open] for the advantage of human development which caused her development of 'Type C Blood' also called 'Boiling Blood'; a mutation responsible for her superhuman regeneration abilities. The exposition to the Classified Materials was also responsible for her hair and eyes turning white, a common side effect after coming in contact with Aether substances.

She is known to be one of the only 3 surviving children that were part of the experiments along with Rosemary and 'La Tragedia'. After this period her life inside the Dempsey Foundation is a mistery being abandoned or having escaped after an unknown period of time.

After The Darkness

Just like her early life, the details of her life during the early months of The Darkness are unknown. It is revealed by members of the Mercenary Union that her begginings as a mercenary were rough, as a loner without any family, friends or home she used to keep a low profile as a guard for resources in exchange for food and a place to sleep while the War Against Beasts took place. Even if she was a mercenary it wasn't until the final weeks of the war that she got to fight, after her strenght and regeneration abilities were noticed by herself and other mercenaries.

Once she got formal training the Dempsey Administrarion developed an interest in her and she was reunited with Anne and Dante Dempsey. She worked for a short period of time inside the Heavy Millitary inside the Woodland's Sky (WS) complex, however, she got back to work as a mercenary but now with constant jobs for New Order. It is during this period where the nickname 'Death Angel' began to be a common nickname for her. It is also during this period where she met Arthur Dempsey thanks to her close history with his parents and agreed to help him train to become a better soldier inside the Heavy Millitary. As Death Angel

Working under the name 'Death Angel' she agreed to help scientist Dr.Cielo del Bosco (nicknamed 'Chel') to investigate the outer borders of the WS borders, a series of expeditions where 'Zaron' (Zachary Jaegermann) was found and taken as a study subject in Chel's charge. While Zaron was being studied by Chel and her colleages Amelia met Yuliana Wattson-Nesterov (nicknamed 'Yuri'), a Russian doctor and nurse inside the WS complex, as Chel became interested in Amelia's abilities and asked for Yuri's help studying her blood.
