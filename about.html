How it all started

On the 10 of December, 2020 Andrew had the idea of developing a game, that's basically how the LONE DAYS project started.
At first it was only going to be a game called Lone Days, however time passed and another 2 projects were created, Broken Heroes and Eternal Darkness which ended up merging together with the Lone Days story in one universe
